Confidence shapes how learners approach challenges. Confident learners are more likely to
attempt difficult tasks, learn from mistakes, and persist through setbacks.

Building confidence requires consistent support, achievable goals, and positive
reinforcement. When learners see progress, even in small steps, motivation increases.

Confidence is not a personality trait it is a product of supportive learning environments.
