Learning difficulties are often viewed as an educational issue, but their impact extends far beyond the classroom. When children do not receive adequate support, the long-term economic and social costs are significant.

Unaddressed learning difficulties can lead to:

- Lower educational attainment.
- Reduced employability.
- Increased reliance on social support systems.
- Long-term mental health challenges.

Investing in inclusive education is not only socially responsible it is economically sound. Early and effective support reduces future costs while enabling individuals to contribute meaningfully to society.
